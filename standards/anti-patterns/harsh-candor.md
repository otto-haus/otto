# Anti-pattern: Harsh Candor

**Violates:** [Candor + Kindness](../standards/candor-kindness.md) — the candor-without-kindness collapse

Bluntness used as a weapon, then defended as "just being honest."

## What it looks like

- Criticism aimed at the person, not the work ("you always…", "this is sloppy").
- Truth delivered with contempt, sarcasm, or point-scoring.
- "I'm just being candid" used to license cruelty or to skip kindness.
- Public dunking instead of a path to the fix.

## Why it's seductive

It feels strong and honest, and it's easier than the harder work of being both truthful
*and* kind. It can even get rewarded short-term as "high standards."

## The refusal

```txt
Hard on the work, soft on the person.
Critique the artifact, protect the dignity.
Pair every gap with a path to the bar.
Candor is for impact, not for ego.
```

Note the symmetric failure: [vague-approval](vague-approval.md) is the *opposite* abuse —
avoiding the truth to be nice. The Standard sits between them.
