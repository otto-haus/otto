09

Motion

Motion is the behavior layer made visible. It is calm, fast, and exact — things resolve into place, they never perform. The same discipline as the rest of the system: rationed, load-bearing, never decoration.

01

Calm over kinetic

Animation states what changed; it does not entertain. If a transition draws attention to itself, cut it. Motion is the quietest way to say a thing moved.

02

Resolve, never bounce

Standard ease-out, no spring, no overshoot. Springy, magical motion is the autopilot fantasy the brand refuses — the same reason "set it and forget it" is banned.

03

Opacity & weight over travel

Prefer a fade or a hairline/weight shift to a slide. When something must move, it travels 4–8px — a settle, not a flight.

04

One emphasis per view

Like the inverted ink block, motion emphasis is spent once. The ratification moment may land with a little weight; everything around it stays still.

Duration & easing tokens

A short scale, mono-set like every other token. Durations climb with the size of the change; nothing exceeds a quarter-second. Easing is shared — one ease-out for everything, no spring anywhere.

Status

Cross-fade, never blink

A file changing state cross-fades its dot and pill hue over 120ms. Status never pulses, flashes, or blinks — a blinking dot is alarm theater, and otto states, it does not perform.

Stage cards

Lift, don't fly

Floating "stage" cards enter at 240ms: opacity 0→1 with a 6px rise and the soft far-cast shadow fading in. The lift mirrors their elevation; the lead-in surfaces stay flat and still.

The ratification moment

Where motion is spent

The inverted ink block is the one place motion carries weight: a 240ms fade with a slight scale settle (0.98→1) as the Receipt is written. Used once per arc — if everything moves, nothing matters.

Icons

Single-stroke draw-on

If an icon animates, its hairline draws on once in ≤200ms (standard ease), consistent with the line language. No spin, no morph, no looping idle.

Accessibility

Honor reduced motion

Under prefers-reduced-motion, every duration collapses to 0ms and transitions become instant state swaps. Calm and exact includes respecting a person who asked for stillness.

Loading

Indeterminate, not anxious

Waiting is a quiet hairline shimmer or a single slow sweep — never a fast spinner. The system is working, not racing; the wait should read as composure.
